Me

Posted on 2018, Apr 20 One minute read

Hi, I’m Adam.

I’m a software engineer who really loves blogs. I, however, really suck at writing. In university I once managed to get 19% on an essay, although I did just about get the grasp of academic writing, in the end, clutching a first on my dissertation module.

I’d love to be able to write well-articulated blog posts that can excite readers about topics I am passionate about, and the only way I am going to get better at that is by doing it.

So this blog is for me to get better at writing technical (and some non-technical) blog posts, and hopefully at some point along the way they will become readable and enjoyable.

If you want to give me some feedback, have any questions, or want to reach out for whatever reason you can contact me on any of the social media platforms in the sidebar.
